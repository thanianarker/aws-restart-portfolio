### Responsible AI Practices

**Overview:**  
This course introduced key principles of **Responsible Artificial Intelligence (AI)** and how to apply them when designing and deploying AI systems on AWS. Topics included identifying bias in AI models, understanding the risks of generative AI, and ensuring transparency and explainability in machine learning systems.

**Key Concepts Learned:**
- Principles and core dimensions of responsible AI
- Identifying and mitigating bias in AI models
- Risks associated with generative AI
- Responsible model selection and dataset preparation
- Transparent and explainable AI models
- Human-centered design for explainable AI

**AWS Services Covered:**
- Amazon Bedrock
- Amazon SageMaker
- Amazon Augmented AI (A2I)
