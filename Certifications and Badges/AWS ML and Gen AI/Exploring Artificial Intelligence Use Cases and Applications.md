### Exploring AI Use Cases and Applications

**Overview:**  
This course explored real-world applications of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and how businesses can use AI and machine learning to solve practical problems. It also covered when AI solutions are appropriate, the capabilities and challenges of generative AI, and key considerations when selecting AI models.

**Key Concepts Learned:**
- Real-world AI and machine learning applications
- Identifying business problems that AI can solve
- When AI and ML solutions are not appropriate
- Machine learning techniques: supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning
- Capabilities and challenges of generative AI
- Factors to consider when selecting generative AI models
- Business metrics used to evaluate generative AI applications
