## Essentials of Prompt Engineering

Completed the Essentials of Prompt Engineering course, developing foundational skills in designing and optimizing prompts for generative AI applications.

**Key Learnings:**
- Core components of effective prompts  
- Negative prompting and prompt structuring techniques  
- Impact of inference parameters on model outputs  
- Prompt engineering strategies including zero-shot, few-shot, and chain-of-thought prompting  
- Best practices for improving prompt performance  
- Identifying common prompt risks and misuse scenarios  

**AWS Services & Tools:**  
Amazon Bedrock, foundation models, prompt design techniques
