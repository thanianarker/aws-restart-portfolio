### Fundamentals of Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence

**Overview:**  
This course introduced the core concepts of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML), including how AI, ML, deep learning, and generative AI are related. It also covered foundational terminology, different types of machine learning models, and how AWS AI services can be used to build real-world AI solutions.

**Key Concepts Learned:**
- Differences between AI, ML, deep learning, and generative AI
- Types of machine learning: supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning
- Neural networks and foundational AI terminology
- Generative AI concepts and foundation models (FMs)
- The lifecycle of foundation models
- Types of foundation models: large language models, diffusion models, and multimodal models

**AWS Services Covered:**
- Amazon Bedrock
- Amazon Comprehend
- Amazon Polly
